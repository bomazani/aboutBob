A little bit about me...

My professional career has spanned a wide range from owning & operating a photo lab and portrait studio to 21 years as a client services specialist at a mutual fund company to most recently, a web developer. I am and have always been interested in growing plants, so now I am looking to change directions once again and focus on my passion for plants. I'm exploring opportunities to work with a local nursery, while also exploring plant photography, bonsai art & posting YouTube videos.

I have always been passionate about plants. Growing up, our house was always filled with potted plants. I still have my mom's ficus benjamina which has to be at least 40 years old. While we had a fairly small yard, we always had a vegetable garden and my parents loved planting unique trees and bushes.

For the last 7 to 10 years I have dabbled in bonsai. I have gathered most of my experience from YouTube videos and good old fashioned trial and error. Throughout varying levels of success, I have developed a small collection of bonsai which I am proud of. I am looking to expand on my collection.
